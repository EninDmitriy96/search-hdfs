XHHLL-FM 97.1 / XEHLL-AM 550 is a combo radio station in Salina Cruz, Oaxaca, Mexico carrying the Los 40 format. ==History== The concession for XEUC-AM was awarded to Eduardo Martínez Celis in 1962. In the 1980s, López Lena bought the station; he changed its calls to XEHLL-AM "Radio Mar" in 1991 and moved it from Tehuantepec to Salina Cruz.
