XHGL-FM is a radio station on 97.7 FM in Mérida, Yucatán. It is owned by Grupo SIPSE and is known as Kiss FM with a pop format. ==History== XHGL received its concession on January 23, 1969. It was owned by Andrés García Lavin, founder of Grupo SIPSE, and was the first FM station in Yucatán, broadcasting with an ERP of 8.925 kW.
