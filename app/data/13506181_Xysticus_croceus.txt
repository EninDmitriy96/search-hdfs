Xysticus croceus is a crab spider in the genus Xysticus found in Nepal, Bhutan, China, Korea, Taiwan, and in Japan,Platnick 2007 where it is called "yami-iro kani-gumo".Xysticus croceus It is about 7 mm long It looks in grass and fallen leaves for prey to eat. It hides under stones in the winter, and is often seen in spring and early summer. ==Footnotes== ==References== * (2007): The world spider catalog, version 8.0. American Museum of Natural History.
