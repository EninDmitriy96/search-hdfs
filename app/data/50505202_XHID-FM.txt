XHID-FM is a radio station on 89.7 FM in Álamo, Veracruz. It is known as Radio K-ñon and carries a Grupera format. ==History== XEID-AM 1230, a 500-watt daytimer, received its concession on May 24, 1978. Power was later raised to 2.5 kW day and 1 kW night, and eventually XEID moved to 990 kHz with 10 kW day and 2.5 kW night. XEID moved to FM in 2012. ==References== Category:Radio stations in Veracruz Category:Radio stations established in 1978
