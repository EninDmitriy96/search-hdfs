XBlock is the SDK for the edX MOOC platform, written in Python, and announced and released publicly on March 14, 2013. It aims to enable the global software development community to participate in the construction of the edX educational platform and the next generation of online and blended courses. ==References== ==External links== * XBlock documentation Category:Free content management systems Category:Free learning management systems Category:Free learning support software Category:Free software programmed in Python Category:Software using the GNU AGPL license Category:Virtual learning environments
