Xanthophyllum subcoriaceum is a plant in the family Polygalaceae. The specific epithet is from the Latin meaning "somewhat leathery", referring to the leaves. ==Description== Xanthophyllum subcoriaceum grows as a shrub or tree up to tall with a trunk diameter of up to .
