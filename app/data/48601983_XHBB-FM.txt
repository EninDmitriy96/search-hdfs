XHBB-FM 101.5/XEBB-AM 600 is a combo radio station in Acapulco, Guerrero. It is owned by Grupo ACIR and carries its La Comadre grupera format. ==History== XEBB received its first concession on January 26, 1953. It was owned by Radio Acapulco, S.A. XEBB added its FM counterpart in 1994 and was consolidated into Radio Integral in 2000. ==References== Category:Radio stations in Guerrero Category:Radio stations established in 1953 Category:Grupo ACIR
