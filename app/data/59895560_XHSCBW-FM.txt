XHSCBW-FM is a community radio station on 100.5 FM in Yuriria, Guanajuato, Mexico. The station is owned by the civil association Yuririapundaro 104.7, A.C. ==History== Yuririapundaro 104.7 filed for a community station on May 4, 2017.
