XHOB-FM is a radio station on 96.1 FM in San Luis Potosí, San Luis Potosí. It is owned by MG Radio and is known as Factor 96.1 with a pop format. ==History== XHOB received its concession on September 20, 1971. It was owned by Benjamin Orozco Barba. Centri Radio acquired it in 1982, and Radiocomunicación Enfocada picked it up in 1992. ==References== Category:Radio stations in San Luis Potosí
