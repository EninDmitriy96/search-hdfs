Abdu Zehi (, also Romanized as ʿAbdū Zehī; also known as ‘Abdī Zā’ī, ‘Abdī Zehī, ‘Abdīzehī, ‘Abdozī, and ‘Abd Zehī) is a village in Polan Rural District, Polan District, Chabahar County, Sistan and Baluchestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 552, in 97 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chabahar County
