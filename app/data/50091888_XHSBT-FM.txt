XHSBT-FM is a radio station on 99.5 FM in San Buenaventura, Chihuahua, Mexico. It is owned by Grupo BM Radio and known as La Que Buena de San Buena. ==History== XHSBT received its concession on October 28, 1994. It was initially owned by Ernesto Agustín Salayandia García.
