Xie Jin (, 1355–1430) was a Chinese landscape painter and calligrapher during the Ming Dynasty (1368-1644). Xie was also known by other names: 'Kongzhao' (孔昭), 'Kuiyin' (葵印), 'Dieshan Xie' (謝疊山), and 'Tingsheng Lan' (蘭庭生). Some of his works can be found at the Shanghai Museum.
