Xodiac (; stylized in all caps) is a South Korean boy band formed by One Cool Jacso. The group currently consists of nine members: Hyunsik, Zayyan, Lex, Beomsoo, Gyumin, Wain, Sing, Davin and Leo. The group made their official debut on April 25, 2023, with the release of the digital single "Throw A Dice". ==Career== ===Pre-debut=== Prior to the group's debut, several members had participated in reality survival shows. Zayyan participated in the shows Rising Star Indonesia in 2018 and Indonesian Idol 2020 in 2019. In 2019, Hyunsik competed in Mnet's survival reality show, Produce X 101. However, he did not make it into the final lineup of the boy group, X1. In 2021, Lex competed on the show Loud under his birth name Zo Doo-hyun. He was eliminated in the final round of the show. ===2023–present: Debut with "Throw A Dice"=== Xodiac made their official debut on April 25, 2023, with the release of their the digital single "Throw A Dice".
