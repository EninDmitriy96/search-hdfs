XHCN-FM is a radio station on 88.5 FM in Irapuato, Guanajuato, Mexico. XHCN is owned by Radio Grupo Antonio Contreras and carries the Los 40 format. ==History== XHCN began as XECN-AM 1080. Owned by Antonio Contreras Hidalgo, XECN received its concession on July 1, 1963. XECN moved to FM in 2011 on 88.5 MHz. ==References== Category:Radio stations in Guanajuato Category:Radio stations established in 1963
