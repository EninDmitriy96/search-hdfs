"Xxplosive" is a song by Dr. Dre from his album 2001. It features Kurupt, Hittman, and Six-Two on the verses and Nate Dogg singing the chorus. The track is based upon the song "Bumpy's Lament" by Soul Mann & the Brothers. The original title was "The Way I Be Pimpin"The Way I Be Pimpin on YouTube which featured Royce da 5'9" on the chorus and Dre rapping the verses. ==Charts== Along with a lot of songs from the 2001 album, the song charted on the Hot R&B;/Hip-Hop Songs due to radio play after the album was released. It reached No. 51.
