XHSOM-FM is a community radio station on 106.9 FM in Tlacolula de Matamoros, Oaxaca. The station is owned by the civil association Somos Uno Radio, La Voz de la Comunidad, A.C. ==History== The station was awarded on August 8, 2018. Somos Uno Radio had been operating as a pirate station since 2015; it relocated to its assigned 106.9 frequency on June 9, 2019. ==References== Category:Radio stations in Oaxaca Category:Community radio stations in Mexico Category:Former pirate radio stations Category:Radio stations established in 2015
