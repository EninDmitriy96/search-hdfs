XHUAA-FM is a university radio station on 94.5 FM in Aguascalientes City, Aguascalientes, Mexico, owned by the Autonomous University of Aguascalientes. ==History== The UAA's radio station started out on AM as XEUAA-AM 1520, debuting on January 13, 1978.
