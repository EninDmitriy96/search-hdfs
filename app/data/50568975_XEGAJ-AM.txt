XEGAJ-AM is a radio station on 790 AM in Guadalajara, Jalisco. It is owned by Radio Fórmula and carries its news/talk programming. ==History== XEGAJ received its concession on April 1, 1992. It was owned by Radio Color, S.A., a Radiorama subsidiary. It was sold to Radio Fórmula in 2000. ==References== Category:Radio stations in Guadalajara Category:Radio Fórmula
