XHUTX-FM is a radio station in Tlaxcala City, Tlaxcala, Mexico. Broadcasting on 99.5 FM from a transmitter on Cerro Ostol, XHUTX is owned by the Autonomous University of Tlaxcala and broadcasts a university radio format known as Radio Universidad. ==History== After obtaining its permit in July 2000, Radio Universidad officially came to air on January 7, 2002, operating from 7am to 10pm. The station currently has programming from 6am to 10pm.
