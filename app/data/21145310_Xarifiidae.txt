Xarifiidae is a family of cyclopoid copepods in the order Cyclopoida. There are about 5 genera and more than 90 described species in Xarifiidae. ==Genera== These five genera belong to the family Xarifiidae: * Botegokika Ho, Cheng & Dai, 2013 * Lipochrus Humes & Dojiri, 1982 * Orstomella H.o.Humes, 1968 * Xarifia Humes, 1960 * Zazaranus Humes & Dojiri, 1983 ==References== Category:Cyclopoida Category:Articles created by Qbugbot Category:Crustacean families
