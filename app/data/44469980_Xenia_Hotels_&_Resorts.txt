Xenia Hotels & Resorts, Inc. is a real estate investment trust that invests in hotels. As of December 31, 2019, it owned 39 hotels comprising 11,245 rooms. ==History== In 2014, the company was formed as a corporate spin-off of InvenTrust Properties. On February 4, 2015, the company became a public company. In December 2016, the company sold 4 hotels for $119 million. In December 2019, the company sold 2 hotels for $61.5 million.
