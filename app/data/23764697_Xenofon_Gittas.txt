Xenofon Gittas (, born 22 June 1979) is a Greek football midfielder who plays for Ethnikos Filippiada. ==Career== Born in Ioannina, Gittas has previously played for PAS Giannena, Panathinaikos, Kerkyra, Kallithea, Panthrakikos, AEK Larnaca, Ilioupoli, Rodos, Paniliakos and Doxa Kranoula .
