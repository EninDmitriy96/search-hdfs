XHPKAS-FM was a radio station on 93.3 FM in Tunkás, Yucatán, known as Ki'in Radio. ==History== XHPKAS was awarded in the IFT-4 radio auction of 2017 to Debate por Yucatán, an independent news site, and was the cheapest station purchased at auction with a final bid of just 5,000 pesos. The station operated from 2018 to 2019.
