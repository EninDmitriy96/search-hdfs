Xiangxi Airport () is an airport under construction to serve Xiangxi Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture in Hunan Province, China. It is located in Huayuan County. The airport will be a dual-use general aviation and commercial aviation airport. Construction of the airport started in 2020 with an investment of CNY 1.67 billion.
