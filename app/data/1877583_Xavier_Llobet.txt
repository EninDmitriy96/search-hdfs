Xavier Llobet Sallent (born May 16, 1974 http://www.triatlo.org/competicions/inscrits.aspx?numero_car=918 in Manresa, Catalonia) is a Spanish triathlete. Llobet competed in the second Olympic triathlon at the 2004 Summer Olympics. He did not finish the competition.
