Xinxiang railway station () is a railway station in Weibin District, Xinxiang, Henan, China. The station is on Beijing–Guangzhou railway, and serves as the eastern terminus of Xinxiang–Yueshan railway and the western terminus of Xinxiang–Yanzhou railway. ==History== The station was established in 1905 as Xinxiang County railway station. ==Station layout== The station has 9 platforms (1 side platform and 4 island platforms) and 10 tracks.
