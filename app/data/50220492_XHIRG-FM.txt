XHIRG-FM is a radio station on 102.7 FM in Irapuato, Guanajuato, Mexico. XHIRG is owned by Radiorama and carries a regional Mexican format known as Campirana. ==History== XHIRG began as XEIRG-AM 1590, a daytimer, in November 1994.
