Xavier Chevrier (; born 17 March 1990) is an Italian male mountain runner and long-distance runner who won 2017 European Mountain Running Championships. ==Biography== Originally from Nus, he also won three national championships at individual senior level in mountain running. He is the cousin of the cross- country skiing champion Federico Pellegrino.
