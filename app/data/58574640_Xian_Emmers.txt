Xian Ghislaine Emmers (born 20 July 1999) is a Belgian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Liga I club Rapid București. ==Club career== He made his Serie B debut for Cremonese on 31 August 2018 in a game against Palermo. On 11 July 2019 Emmers joined Belgian side Waasland-Beveren on year- long deal. On 8 August 2020 went to Almere City on loan.
