XHUDEM-FM is a radio station serving Monterrey, Nuevo León. It is owned by the Universidad de Monterrey and broadcasts on 90.5 FM from its campus in San Pedro Garza García. ==History== XHUMY-FM was permitted on February 11, 1994 and had an effective radiated power of 10 watts. The university changed the callsign to XHUDM-FM in 1996 and to the current XHUDEM-FM in 2000. ==References== Category:Spanish-language radio stations Category:Radio stations in Monterrey Category:University radio stations in Mexico Category:Radio stations established in 1994 Category:1994 establishments in Mexico
