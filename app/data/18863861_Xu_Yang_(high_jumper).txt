Xu Yang (born 27 June 1970 in Jiangsu) is a retired Chinese high jumper. He won the silver medals at the 1993 and 1995 Asian Championships and the bronze medal at the 1994 Asian Games. He also competed at the 1991 World Championships and the 1992 Olympic Games without reaching the final.
