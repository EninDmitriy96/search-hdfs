Xenocytaea vonavonensis is a jumping spider species in the genus Xenocytaea. The male was first identified in 2011 by Barbara Maria Patoleta. The female has not been described. ==Description== The species has a brown cephalothorax with iridescent scales, which is long. ==Distribution== Xenocytaea vonavonensis is found on Vonavona in the Solomon Islands, after which the species is named. ==References== Category:Fauna of the Solomon Islands Category:Salticidae Category:Spiders described in 2011 Category:Spiders of Oceania
