Xaa-Pro dipeptidase (, prolidase, imidodipeptidase, proline dipeptidase, peptidase D, gamma-peptidase) is an enzyme. This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction : Hydrolysis of Xaa!Pro dipeptides; also acts on aminoacyl-hydroxyproline analogs This enzyme is Mn2+-activated. == References == == External links == * Category:EC 3.4.13
