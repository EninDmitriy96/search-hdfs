XHMMF-FM is a radio station on 92.3 FM in Mexicali, Baja California, Mexico. The station is owned by Grupo Audiorama Baja California and carries its La Bestia Grupera grupera format. ==History== XHMMF received its first concession on February 11, 1980. The station had been proposed as early as 1964.
