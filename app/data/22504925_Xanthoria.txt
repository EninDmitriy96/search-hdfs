Xanthoria is a genus of lichenized fungi in the family Teloschistaceae. Common names include orange lichen,Xanthoria. USDA PLANTS. orange wall lichen,Field Guide to California Lichens, Stephen Sharnoff, Yale University Press, 2014, and sunburst lichen.
