XERNB-AM is a radio station on 1450 AM in Sahuayo, Michoacán, Mexico. It is owned by Promoradio and known as Radio Impacto. ==History== left|thumb|180px|XERNB-AM logo until 2019 XEGC-AM received its concession on March 13, 1948. It was owned by Alberto Barragán Degollado and sold to José Raúl Nava Becerra in 1982. The callsign was changed to XERNB-AM in the early 2000s.
