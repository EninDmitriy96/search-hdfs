Xiao Chen Tao (小臣妥; died in the 1100s BCE), was a Chinese princess, the daughter of king Wu Ding.Thorp, Robert L. (2006). China in the Early Bronze Age: Shang Civilization. University of Pennsylvania Press. . Wu Ding granted Tao her own personal fief, and elevated her to a ministerial position, xiao chen, by which she is now referred.
