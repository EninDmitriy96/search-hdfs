XHPLEO-FM is a radio station on 88.5 FM in Huajuapan de León, Oaxaca. It is owned by the Vera Hernández family and is known as La GranDiosa de Huajuapan. ==History== XHPLEO was awarded in the IFT-4 radio auction of 2017 and came to air in April 2018.
