XHOZ-FM is a radio station in Querétaro, Querétaro. Broadcasting on 94.7 FM, XHOZ is owned by Grupo Imagen and carries its Imagen Radio network. ==History== The concession was issued to René Olivares Gascón in 1982. It was sold to Impulsora de Radiodifusión, S.A., and then to Imagen. ==References== Category:Radio stations in Querétaro Category:Grupo Imagen
