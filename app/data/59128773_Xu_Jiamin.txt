Xu Jiamin (; born 11 April 1994) is a Chinese footballer who currently plays as a goalkeeper for Chinese Super League side Tianjin Jinmen Tiger. ==Club career== Xu Jiamin started his professional football career in 2011 when he was part of the Shanghai Zobon's squad for the 2011 China League Two campaign.2011年中乙联赛完全名单之上海中邦足球俱乐部 at sohu.com 2011-05-16. He would be used as reserve goalkeeper and eventually go on to make his debut in a Chinese FA Cup game on 7 July 2015 against Jiangxi Liansheng that ended in a 3-2 victory.
