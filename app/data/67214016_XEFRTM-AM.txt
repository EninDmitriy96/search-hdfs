XEFRTM-AM is a radio station on 770 AM in Fresnillo, Zacatecas, Mexico. It is owned by GlobalMedia and is known as W Radio with programming from W Radio. The transmitter site is located southwest of Zacatecas. ==History== XEFRTM was awarded in the IFT-4 radio auction of 2017 and is one of three stations GlobalMedia companies obtained in the auction.
