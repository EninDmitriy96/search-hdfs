XHBAK-FM is a radio station in Bachajón, Chilón, Chiapas. It broadcasts on 98.7 FM and is known as Radio Ach' Lequilc'op, broadcasting programs principally in Tzeltal to serve the local Mayan indigenous community. ==History== The station launched on March 21, 2015, after receiving its permit in September 2014. It was originally held by a civil association, Comunicación Educativa y Cultural Bats'il K'op, A.C. In February 2017, in order to transition the station to a social indigenous concession, the station was transferred to direct control by the indigenous community. ==References== Category:Radio stations in Chiapas Category:Community radio stations in Mexico Category:Indigenous radio stations in Mexico Category:Radio stations established in 2015
