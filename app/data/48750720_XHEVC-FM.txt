XHEVC-FM is a radio station on 104.5 FM in Fortín de las Flores, Veracruz, México. It is owned by Radio Cañón and carries the Ke Buena grupera format from Radiópolis. ==History== XHEVC received its concession on April 20, 1960, as XEVC-AM, broadcasting on 700 kHz. It was owned by Carlos Ferraez Matos. It migrated to FM in 2011. In 2021, NTR acquired the ABC Radio group from Organización Editorial Mexicana. The station was known as La Más Guapa, La Guapachosa from 2022 to 2023. On April 23, 2023, as part of a national alliance between the company and Radiópolis, 22 NTR-owned stations adopted franchise formats from Radiópolis, with XHEVC switching to the Ke Buena grupera format. ==References== Category:Radio stations in Veracruz
