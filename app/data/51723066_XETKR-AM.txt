XETKR-AM is a radio station on 1480 AM in Monterrey, Nuevo León, Mexico, licensed in Guadalupe. It is owned by Multimedios Radio and is known as TKR. ==History== XENJ-AM received its concession on March 21, 1961. It operated from Guadalupe and was owned by Remigio González González. Radio Triunfos, S.A. became the concessionaire in July 1964, with the callsign changing to the current XETKR.
