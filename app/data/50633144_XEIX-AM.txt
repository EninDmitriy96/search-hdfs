XEIX-AM is a radio station on 1290 AM in Jiquilpan, Michoacán, Mexico. It is owned by Promoradio and known as Super Éxitos. ==History== XEIX received its concession on June 22, 1965. It was owned by José Luis Lemus Orozco. It is the sister to a station in Sahuayo, XERNB-AM 1450. ==References== Category:Radio stations in Michoacán Category:Radio stations established in 1965
