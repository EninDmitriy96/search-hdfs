A Vacation in Hell is a 1979 American made-for-television, action-adventure- thriller film starring Priscilla Barnes, Barbara Feldon, Maureen McCormick, Andrea Marcovicci, and Michael Brandon as part of a group of lost vacationers being stalked through the Hawaiian jungle. It was helmed by Roots director David Greene and has acquired a cult following, due in large part to subsequent reairings in syndication. ==Cast== *Priscilla Barnes ... Denise *Barbara Feldon ...
