XHECM-FM (branded as Bonita) is a Mexican Spanish-language FM radio station in Ciudad Mante, Tamaulipas. ==History== XECM-AM 1450 received its concession on November 9, 1951. The station was owned by Ricardo López Méndez and broadcast with 1,000 watts day and 250 night.
